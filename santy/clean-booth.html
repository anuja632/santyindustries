Clean Booth Solutions

Engineered Clean Booth

A clean booth, often used in industries requiring a controlled environment, utilizes Fan Filter Units (FFUs) to maintain a high level of air cleanliness. FFUs are self-contained units that integrate a fan, motor, and filters, specifically HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) or ULPA (Ultra-Low Particulate Air) filters, to purify air and create a laminar airflow pattern within the booth. Our clean booths provide controlled environments with efficient airflow, contamination control, and compliance with ISO cleanroom standards.
